### The 12 Days to Innovation

!!! note "Inspired by Donella Meadows"
    This series builds on the systems thinking tradition of Donella Meadows' *Thinking in Systems* and her [twelve leverage points for intervention](https://donellameadows.org/archives/leverage-points-places-to-intervene-in-a-system/). Each day explores how the principles of Digital Physics—mass, heat, entropy, gravity—apply to trust systems, starting from the foundational "Thermostat Fallacy" and ascending to "The Edge of the Map." Like Meadows' leverage points, these concepts progress from shallow interventions to deep structural changes in how we build resilient systems.
